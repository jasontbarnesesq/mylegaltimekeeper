What is My Legal Timekeeper?

My Legal Timekeeper is a voice-enabled time tracking application designed for legal professionals, consultants, and anyone who needs to track billable hours efficiently. Instead of filling out tedious forms, simply speak your time entries naturally and let the app handle the rest.

How It Works

    Start a new entry: Click the microphone button to use voice input or switch to manual mode for typing
    Provide details: Answer simple questions about the client, matter, time type, work performed, and hours spent
    Review entries: See all your entries for today with running totals and detailed breakdowns
    Export data: Click "Export CSV" to download your time records in a format compatible with billing systems
    Optional: Connect Google Drive to automatically save exports to the cloud for safekeeping

üîí Privacy & Data Security

Your data stays yours. All time entries are stored locally in your browser using secure browser storage. If you choose to connect Google Drive, files are saved only to your personal Google Drive account that you control.

We never:

    Access your data without permission
    Share your information with third parties
    Sell your data
    Track your activity across other websites

Google Drive Integration (Optional)

When you enable Google Drive integration, the app will:

    Request permission to create files in your Google Drive (you can revoke this anytime in your Google Account settings)
    Automatically save CSV exports to your Google Drive for backup and easy access
    Use your email address for authentication purposes only

What we DON'T do: We never access files you didn't explicitly create with this app. We don't read your emails, calendar, photos, or any other Google data. The app only has permission to create new files that you explicitly export.

Why Voice-Enabled?

Traditional time tracking requires switching between tasks, opening forms, and typing details. With voice input, you can quickly log time entries by speaking naturally while the app handles the data entry. This means:

    Less interruption to your workflow
    Faster entry - speaking is quicker than typing
    More accurate narratives - natural speech captures more detail
    Easier end-of-day logging - just talk through your day
